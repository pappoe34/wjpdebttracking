How it works

Three steps. No catch.

1

Get your code

Sign in to WJP. Your unique referral code lives in Settings → Referrals. Each user has one — they're tied to your account.

2

Share it

Give friends your code or share the link. Anyone who signs up using it is attributed to you for 30 days.

3

Earn

When 5 of those friends activate paid subscriptions, we comp you a full year of Pro Plus. $299 value.

Fine print — read before you share

  • Paid only. Sign-ups don't count. Only referred users who activate a paid Pro or Pro Plus subscription count toward your 5.
  • 30-day clawback. If a referred user cancels within 30 days of activating, that referral doesn't count. We don't reward cancellations.
  • One reward per cycle. Earning a year resets your counter. You can earn another year by referring 5 more.
  • No self-referrals. Same payment method, same household, same IP — we screen for these and disqualify them silently.
  • Trial referrals don't count. Only the activated paid subscription after the trial counts.
  • Reward is service credit, not cash. Free Pro Plus year, not money. Non-transferable, no cash equivalent.
