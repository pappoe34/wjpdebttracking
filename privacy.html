Privacy Policy

Effective: April 22, 2026

WJP Debt Tracking ("WJP," "we," "us"), a service of Pappoe Venture LLC, helps you connect your debt accounts and engineer a payoff plan. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have.

The short version. We collect the minimum needed to run the app — your email and the financial-account data Plaid returns when you link an institution. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. You can delete your account and all associated data from inside the app at any time.

1. Who we are

WJP Debt Tracking is operated by Pappoe Venture LLC, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of New Jersey, United States. Contact: privacy@wjpdebttracking.com.

2. Information we collect

Account information

  • Your email address (provided when you sign up with Google or email/password)
  • A Firebase user ID (an internal identifier we generate to look up your data)

Financial data via Plaid

When you link a financial institution through Plaid, we receive and store:

  • Account names, types, and masked account numbers (last 4 digits only)
  • Account balances for liabilities (credit cards, loans, lines of credit)
  • Loan and credit metadata (APR, minimum payment, due date, statement balance)
  • Recent transactions, used to automatically detect and apply payments to your debt balances
  • Recurring-transaction patterns, used to flag autopays on your debt cards

We do not receive or store your bank login credentials. Those are handled by Plaid directly. See Plaid's End User Privacy Policy for how Plaid handles your information.

Usage data

Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) generated by our hosting providers for security and reliability. We do not run third-party advertising or analytics trackers.

3. How we use your information

  • To display your debts, balances, and progress inside the app
  • To match incoming transactions against your debts so balances stay current automatically
  • To tag debts that are on autopay so you can see at a glance what's covered
  • To send you transactional emails (password resets, security notices)
  • To diagnose bugs and keep the service running

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale.

4. Who we share data with

We share data only with the service providers we need to operate the app:

  • Plaid — to connect your financial accounts and retrieve balance and transaction data
  • Google Firebase / Google Cloud — for authentication, database storage (Firestore), and serverless infrastructure
  • Netlify — for website and serverless function hosting

Each of these providers is contractually bound to handle your data according to their own privacy and security commitments. We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

We may disclose information if required by law (court order, subpoena, or other valid legal process).

5. How we protect your data

  • In transit: TLS 1.2+ on every request (HTTPS only)
  • At rest: AES-256 encryption applied by default by Google Cloud Firestore
  • Access controls: Per-user Firestore security rules so each account can only read its own data
  • Plaid tokens: Stored server-side only and never exposed to the browser
  • Webhooks: Cryptographically verified (JWT signature check) before being processed
  • Admin access: Two-factor authentication enforced on all administrative accounts

6. Data retention and deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can:

  • Disconnect a single institution from inside the app — this revokes the Plaid access token for that institution and removes its accounts and transactions from your dashboard.
  • Delete your entire account from your account-management screen — this removes all of your Firestore documents, revokes every Plaid item token via Plaid's /item/remove endpoint, and signs you out permanently.

Deletion is irreversible. We may retain limited records for up to 90 days for legal, security, or backup purposes, after which they are purged.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR (EU), the CCPA/CPRA (California), or similar state laws to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Receive a portable copy of your data

You can exercise the deletion right directly inside the app. For any other request, email privacy@wjpdebttracking.com and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Children

WJP Debt Tracking is not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International users

Our infrastructure is hosted in the United States. If you are accessing the service from outside the U.S., your data will be transferred to and processed in the U.S.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated by email or via an in-app notice before they take effect.
