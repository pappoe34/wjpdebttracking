Questions

Fair questions. Honest answers.

Is WJP really free?

Yes — the core planner, tracker, and AI co-pilot are free forever. A future Pro tier adds automated payment scheduling and tax-optimized debt payoff — but you'll never need Pro to get out of debt.

Do you connect to my bank?

Optionally. You can type balances in manually and WJP works perfectly. If you link your bank, we use read-only connections via Plaid — we can never move money, and we never store your login.

Snowball vs. avalanche — which should I use?

Avalanche saves more money mathematically. Snowball feels more motivating because you clear small balances first. WJP shows you both — and many people use a hybrid. There's no wrong answer if you stick to the plan.

What about my data?

It's yours. We use bank-grade encryption at rest and in transit, we don't sell, and we don't advertise. You can export everything as CSV or delete your account (and all data) in one click.

Who's behind WJP?

An independent team — not a bank, not a lender, not a BNPL company. We make money from a single optional Pro tier. That's our only business model, and it keeps our incentives aligned with yours.
