How it works

Three steps. Real freedom.

No spreadsheets. No jargon. You're on a plan in under four minutes — and everything after runs on autopilot.

Link your banks, credit cards, student loans, auto loans, and mortgages through Plaid — the same secure bridge used by Venmo, Robinhood, and Coinbase. No passwords stored on our servers. No way for us to move money. Read-only, end-to-end encrypted.

  • Takes under 3 minutes from the first click to every balance visible
  • Supports 12,000+ US financial institutions out of the box
  • Auto-refreshes balances and transactions in real time
  • Can't see: your PIN, your password, or your security questions

2

Pick a plan. Or let AI pick one.

Snowball clears small balances first for psychological momentum. Avalanche kills highest APR first for maximum math savings. AI Hybrid runs thousands of simulations against your actual cash flow and adapts month to month. Every plan ships with an exact debt-free date and the total interest you dodge.

  • See all three strategies side by side — no guessing
  • Payoff date updates live as balances and rates change
  • Switch strategies anytime — WJP re-runs the math in under a second
  • Works with any mix of debt: cards, loans, BNPL, mortgage
3

Let it run in the background.

Reminders fire before bills drop. Transactions are auto-matched to the right debt the moment they clear. If a payment slips, WJP rebalances the plan without a guilt trip. You can tune exactly what you want to see — or mute everything and let it just work.

  • Bill reminders 3, 2, and 1 day before due — configurable per account
  • Auto-matching catches every payment, with a 30-second undo window
  • Credit utilization alerts when a card crosses 30% — before your score reacts
  • Weekly email digest: progress, wins, drift, next week's targets
