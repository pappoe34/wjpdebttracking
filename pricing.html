Common pricing questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — one click in Stripe's portal. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No clawback, no auto-charge after cancellation.

What happens after the 14-day Pro trial?

On day 14 your card is charged for the next month (or year, if annual). We email you 3 days before so you have time to cancel without surprise.

Can I switch between Pro and Pro Plus?

Yes — Stripe portal handles upgrades and downgrades, prorating the difference. You only pay the gap.

What if I want a refund?

Email support@wjpdebttracking.com within 30 days of any charge. Full refund, no questions asked.

Do you sell my data?

Never. No advertising, no data brokers. Your subscription is our only revenue source — that's the whole point of this pricing model.

What if I downgrade back to Free?

All your manually-entered debts and transactions stay. Plaid syncs and AI Coach Cloud Mode get disabled until you re-upgrade.

Why no free trial on Pro Plus?

Pro Plus is for people who already love the product. Start Pro free for 14 days; if you hit a Pro Plus-only feature (household mode, unlimited banks/AI), upgrade with a prorated charge.

Is there an annual discount?

Yes. Pro is $99/yr (≈$8.25/mo, save 31%). Pro Plus is $199/yr (≈$16.58/mo, save 33%). Toggle "Yearly" above to see the lower prices.
