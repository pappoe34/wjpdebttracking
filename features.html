Auto-matched payments

Every payment, in the right bucket.

The moment a payment clears, WJP figures out which debt it went to and decrements the balance. Your plan stays current in real time — no manual reconciliation, no missed payments falling through the cracks. Miss something? 30-second undo window.

  • Real-time via Plaid transaction webhooks
  • Handles automatic minimums AND extra payments
  • Catches "mystery" deposits and credits too
  • Re-balances your payoff date the instant a payment lands

Bank-level security

256-bit encryption. Read-only Plaid. Never store passwords.

+

We built security the way a bank would — then added the layers a bank wouldn't bother with.

  • TLS 1.3 in transit — every connection between your device and ours is encrypted.
  • AES-256 at rest — the industry standard used by US financial institutions.
  • Plaid read-only connections — same secure bridge used by Venmo, Coinbase, Robinhood.
  • Zero password storage — we literally cannot see or reuse your bank login.
  • Optional MFA — add a second factor to your WJP account in Settings.
Privacy first

Your data is yours. No selling, no advertisers, no resale. Ever.

+

Our business model pays us when you pay off debt — not when advertisers pay us for your data.

  • No data sales — not to banks, not to advertisers, not to aggregators. Not even "anonymized."
  • No ad trackers — no Facebook Pixel, no Google Analytics on authenticated pages.
